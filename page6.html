Health Care

The students of UCS can avail free medical aid from the University Health Centre. There are specialists attached to the Centre who offer expert medical advice.

The Health Centre is located near the Krishnaveni Hostel (B - Hostel).

Operating Hours

The Health Centre operates from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon and 4:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. on all working days.

Medical Staff

Several physicians, headed by the Chief Medical Officer and including a lady doctor, manage the Health Centre. It has a well-equipped pathology lab, a physiotherapy unit, dispensary, etc., and provides out-patient treatment only.

Visiting specialist doctors are available on scheduled days for expert advice.
