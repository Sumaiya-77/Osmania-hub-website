Physical Education

The Department of Physical Education has been part of the Arts College since its inception. Initially, games and sports activities were centralized for all campus colleges. Since 1975, the Arts College has been organizing indoor games such as Table Tennis, Carroms, Chess, and Badminton within the college premises. For outdoor games, students use the facilities of the main Department of Physical Education, Osmania University.
