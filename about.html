University College of Science

About Us

The University College of Science at Osmania University is one of its oldest and most prestigious colleges. Established in 1918, it initially started with four departments and has since expanded to include 14 regular departments and 4 self-financing courses. The college offers a variety of postgraduate programs, including M.Sc. and Ph.D. courses in multiple disciplines.

Presently, the college has 2000 students pursuing their PG courses in different subjects. Students for the M.Sc. course are admitted through the Entrance Test conducted by Osmania University.

The college is known for its strong emphasis on research and has a dedicated placement cell to assist students in securing employment. It also provides hostel facilities for both male and female students.

The University College of Science offers a Ph.D. program in 16 subjects. Students are admitted into the Ph.D. program based on their performance in the Entrance Test and interview. The students are accommodated in the Maneru, Krishnaveni (B), and New PG Hostels. The girl's students are accommodated in the Ladies Hostel. All hostels are equipped with the necessary infrastructure facilities to cater to the needs of the students.
